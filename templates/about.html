About BGS and SJB Group of Institutions

BGS and SJB Group of Institutions, located in Kengeri, Bengaluru, Karnataka, India, is dedicated to excellence in education, research, and holistic development. The group offers a wide range of academic programs and world-class facilities, nurturing talent and fostering innovation.

Vision

To impart quality education and foster holistic development, empowering students to become leaders and innovators who contribute positively to society.

Mission

To nurture talent, promote research, and serve society by providing a dynamic learning environment, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a commitment to excellence.
